who are we?

We are fresh plants and flower sellers. Having plants in our homes or in our offices doesn't just look good, it also boosts our mood, makes us more productive, and cleans the air around us by absorbing toxins. Most of us being urban dwellers spending their days in apartments with limited access to parks and ecological reserves, have no way of feeling close to nature and experiencing the benefits of being around plants.

So here, we choose different vendors from different areas with wide variety of plants and flowers, sell them through our website and delivering the product to the customers at their door step.These will improve the sales of small vendors and also make customers view a wide range of collection.
